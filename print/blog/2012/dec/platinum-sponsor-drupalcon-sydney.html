Cross(Functional) is proud to be a platinum sponsor for DrupalCon Sydney [1] Feb 6-9 2013. DrupalCon Sydney is the first DrupalCon to held outside the U.S and Europe. The conference will be held in in Crown Plaza [2], Coogee and the general conference tickets have already sold out (450) two months prior to its commence.

Here a breakdown of the conference's program:

Wed 6 Feb

  • Training - three training sessions [3] are available.
  • Business Day [4] - aimed at business leaders and managers who are exploring incorporating Drupal into their technology suite.

Thurs 7 Feb - Fri 8 Feb

During these two days the conference will consists of keynotes, sessions and BOF The keynotes speakers will be Dries Buytaert [5], Drupal’s founder and project lead and Senator Kate Lundy [6], Minister Assisting for Innovation and Industry. The sessions will be from the following tracks [7]:

  • Content Authoring [8]
  • Case Studies [9]
  • Business and Strategy [10]
  • Community [11]
  • Frontend [12]
  • Site Building [13]
  • Coding and Development [14]
  • Core Conversations [15]

You can see the list of the featured [16] and regular sessions [17] on the DrupalCon Sydney website. [7]

Sat 9 Feb

The last day of the conference will consist of a code sprint [18]. A Community Tools Workshop [19] will be held in the morning to assist for those new to code sprints with setting up tools required for a core sprint. Everyone regardless of their Drupal or coding experience is encouraged to come along.

CrossFunctional is also proud to announce that members of our own staff, Ryan Cross and Geoffrey Roberts will be presenting a session at DruaplCon Sydney called Real-Time Mobile Ecommerce in Drupal [20]. This session is a case study that looks Luna Park’s has implementation of it its real-time, mobile e-commerce system built on open, web-standards.
